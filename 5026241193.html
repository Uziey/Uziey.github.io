About Denimitup

Denimitup (by HAM! Jeansku) is an Indonesian local fashion brand known for transforming denim into wearable art, specializing in handcrafted jeans, batik patterns, and unique stitching. Founded by Vincent Mikhael, the brand has expanded internationally with over 100,000 pieces sold, focusing on blending local Indonesian craftsmanship with modern, sustainable denim styles.
